Why We Built Vendo

Before starting Vendo, we worked with more than 300 businesses across all industries and sizes to help them get more out of their analytics and martech tools.

Over time, one disturbing pattern appeared. Most businesses get stuck implementing these tools. What seems to be a relatively straightforward task could take months, and in some cases, it’s never fully implemented.

Vendo was created to solve this problem. We provide an optimized tool that connects your data from other tools and platforms directly to Mixpanel. Faster, cheaper, and better than any alternative.
